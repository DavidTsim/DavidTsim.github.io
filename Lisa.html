About Lisa

She is known by her stage name Lisa, and is now a member of YG Entertainment's girl group BLACKPINK. She is the main dancer, lead rapper and sub-singer in the team. She is also the first foreign artist to debut since the agency was established. On September 10, 2021, Lisa made her solo debut with the single album "LALISA" and achieved her first solo debut in South Korea, becoming the first female artist to exceed 700,000 sales, and won two Guinness World Records.
